Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope. Simple projects take 1-2 weeks, while complex projects can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after the discovery phase.

Do you offer unlimited revisions?

Unlimited revisions are included in our Professional and Enterprise plans. The Starter plan includes up to 2 rounds of revisions.

What's your payment process?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. We accept all major payment methods.

Can you work with existing assets?

Absolutely! We can incorporate your existing brand materials, photos, videos, and other assets into new projects.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, all plans include email support. We offer optional maintenance and support packages for long-term partnerships.

What if I need to change my project scope?

We're flexible! If project scope changes, we'll adjust the timeline and pricing accordingly and keep you informed every step of the way.
